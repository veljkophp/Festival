About Bugs and Heroes

  • Rating:
    3.3 out of 5
  • Duration: 90 min
  • Release Date: 12 April 2018 (Serbia)
  • Genre: Drama
  • Director: Petar Pašić
  • Producer: Petar Jevtić
  • Cast: Bojan Dimitrijević, Slobodan ‘Boda’ Ninković, Zoran Cvijanović


In a world full of fear, where love is slowly drifting away and becoming something unreachable, we come across unfortunate people, such as Raša and his demanding sick father. Raša becomes a guard in an old hotel, and stumbles upon strange characters with whom he shares misfortune.
In the meantime, in a world beneath us, bugs are going through similar problems of loving and loosing...
